FAQ

What exactly is “cost-transparent” pricing?

You see a line-item view of every pilot expense (equipment, software, labor, travel). We add a small operational margin (5–10%) to keep the program running. No hidden fees.

What if the pilot doesn’t meet ROI targets?

If the agreed ROI target isn’t reached, we can waive our margin. You only cover actual costs.

Do we have to commit long-term?

No. Pilots run 8–12 weeks. After the ROI review, you can scale, extend, or exit.
